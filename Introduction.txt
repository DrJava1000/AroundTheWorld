Resident Evil 1: Text Game 
Welcome. You can proceed through the Spencer Mansion based on cardinal directions with 'move'.
You can view, pick up and drop items with inspect, get and remove but you are limited 
to movement and item operations alone.
Commands, directions, and items are typed out and aren't case sensitive(eg. NORTH, north, NoRTh). 
Enter exit to quit the game when you are done.
